How to play the game 😍:

  1. The game always starts with player one.
  2. The game starts when the player rolls the dice by pressing the roll dice button.
  3. A random number appears between 1 and 6 and it's added to the player's current points.
  4. The current points increase each time the player throws the dice.
  5. The player loses his current points if the dice with number 1 appears and the role moves to the other player.
  6. The player can keep his points if he presses the hold button.
  7. The winner is whoever collects 100 points first.
